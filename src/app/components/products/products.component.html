Helix

Indigenous and end-to-end encrypted instant messaging application

Helix is a mobile application developed for engaging in instant end-to-end communication through text, voice and video. It has support for sharing of documents and locations. Additionally, it allows the creation of an unlimited number of groups for staying connected and engaging in conference calls. It is simple to use, with an intuitive interface that users are familiar with. Traditional messaging platforms like Whatsapp, Signal, and Telegram, while widely used, pose risks to national security due to their reliance on foreign entities. Recognizing the importance of independence from third parties and foreign nation-states, we developed Helix to remove the dependencies on these entities, thereby guaranteeing the privacy of sensitive communications.
